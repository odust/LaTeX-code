$Corollary.~Given~G~is~a~group~with~|G|=4, G\equiv \Bbb{Z}_4\lor G\equiv Klein~4-group~V.$\\
$Proof:$\\
$(i)$\\
$G\coloneqq\{e, a, b, c\}, if~\forall x\in G^*\coloneqq G\setminus\{e\}, |x|=2.~Claim~ab=c.~Otherwise, ab=e, b=eb=a^2b=a(ab)=a, C!.~Analogously, ba=c, ac=b=ca, bc=a=cb, hence~G\cong V.$\\
$(ii)$\\
$If~\exists x\in G^*~s.t.~|x|\neq2.~\because|x|\mid|G|=4, \therefore|x|=4, G=<x>\cong\Bbb{Z}_4.$\\
